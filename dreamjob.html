What Is My Dream Job In Information Technology

Overview

My dream job is to one day be a commanding officer of a submarine within the Royal Australian Navy. Being a commander on a submarine is a mentally challenging position that involves being responsible for a multi-million dollar asset as well as the lives of the crew. I would be responsible for ensuring that both the crew and submarine were kept in war-fighting order, including weapons, mechanical and electrical systems, food and logistic supplies.

This job appeals to me as I already live a very structured life and would include very little shift in the way I live my life. I am currently responsible for a small workshop and its staff, taking responsibility for 60 crew members and an expensive piece of equipment is something that would provide me with great pride and a sense of purpose.

Skills, Qualifications and Expierence Required

The career path to become a submarine commander starts with becoming a Marine Warfare Office (MWO) Submariner. To gain entry into this entry level job in the ADF I am required to be medically and physically fit, have a degree, pass an aptitude test, pass a selection board interview and be capable of obtaining a NV1 security clearance.

After gaining the job and passing all initial and job training I will have to spend about 18 years working on a submarine and conducting further specific training through the ADF as well as being competitive for promotive by upholding the values of the Royal Australian Navy.

My Current Skills, Qualifications and Expierence

I already far exceed the required medical and fitness benchmarks required to gain entrance into the Royal Australian Navy. I have already attended a recruitment off and passed the required aptitude test and hold a NV1 security clearance. Having already worked in the Australian Army I believe I already have the knowledge and ability to pass the selection board interview.

How I Plan To Achieve the Skills, Qualifications And Experience I Lack

I currently only hold a Certificate III in Heavy Automotive Technology, but have enrolled in a Bachelor of Information Technology through RMIT to achieve the required level of education for entry into the Navy as a MWO. Once I have gained entry I will accrue the required experience over the course of my career in the hopes of becoming a submarine commander.
